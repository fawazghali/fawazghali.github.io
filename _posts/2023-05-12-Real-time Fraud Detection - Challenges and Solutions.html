--- layout: post title: Real-time Fraud Detection - Challenges and Solutions location: Berlin, Germany host: EIC 2023 --- Abstract:
Fraud can be considerably reduced via speed, scalability, and stability. Investigating fraudulent activities, using fraud detection machine learning is crucial where decisions need to be made in microseconds, not seconds or even milliseconds. This becomes more challenging when things get demanding and scaling real-time fraud detection becomes a bottleneck. The talk will address these issues and provide solutions using the Hazelcast Open Source platform. Resources:
