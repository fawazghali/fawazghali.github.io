--- layout: post title: Real-time Stream Processing without Migraines location: GeoPython 2023 host: GeoPython 2023 --- Abstract:
As businesses grow, many applications will at some point run into scaling issues — what works fine at low transaction, volumes don’t work well, or at all, at high volumes. Real-time technology solutions like the open-source Hazelcast Platform have helped many businesses overcome their scaling issues to provide high throughput and low latency at tremendous scale. In this talk, we’ll focus on how Real-time compute APIs help leverage the processing capabilities of a distributed cluster, so you aren’t leaving large potential performance gains on the table. The combination of Real-time storage and computing provides a unique synergy that enables applications to address real-time use cases at any scale. Resources:
